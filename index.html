About

Developing & maximising potential.

At Oakland we are experts at being in the right place at the right time, unlocking development opportunities and then maximizing their potential.

We typically consider sites of anywhere between 1 to 50 units for all types of tenure including mixed-use, whether it is unconditional, subject to planning or benefitting from an existing planning consent.

Safeguarding the environment

It is estimated that within the next 20 years, we will need over 5 million new homes to accommodate our ever-growing population, but whilst doing this we must also do everything we can to preserve our precious landscape.

Implementing nature conservation policies means we can create wildlife habitats that safeguard our flora and fauna. At the same time, it provides a recreational environment that improves the quality of life for all concerned.
